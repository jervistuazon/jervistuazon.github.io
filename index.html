About

I’m Jervis, a Senior Visual Artist with over 12 years of collective experience in the design industry. For the last 7 years as a Senior Visual Artist at Parks + Associates, I have focused on architectural visualization, producing high-fidelity renders and cinematic animations that help clients see the project clearly before it’s built. Prior to this, I spent 5 years as a 2D Designer at HBA Clark Philippines, where I cultivated a sharp eye for composition and detail.

Currently, I am delving deeper into the future of the craft—integrating AI into visual workflows, exploring dynamic presentation methods, and developing custom tools to streamline the design process. My goal is to bridge traditional artistry with emerging technology, creating deeper, more reliable narratives for every project.

Whether through real-time walkthroughs or bespoke visualization tools, my approach remains grounded: keep the design intent honest, make the visuals feel believable, and deliver efficiently under real production constraints.

Services & Expertise

  • Architectural Visualization High-end marketing still renders for exterior and interior developments, composed in SketchUp, rendered with 3Ds Max and V-Ray, then polished with Photoshop and AI.
  • Cinematic Animation Story-driven walkthroughs that capture the mood of the space, produced using Twinmotion, D5, and Premiere Pro.
  • Real-time Interactive Immersive design reviews and interactive solutions powered by Twinmotion and custom personal tools.
