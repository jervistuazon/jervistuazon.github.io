About

My practice focuses on architectural visualization across high-fidelity renders, cinematic animations, and real-time presentations. Recently, I've been exploring AI-assisted workflows and custom tools to push speed and storytelling.

I'm Jervis, a Senior Visual Artist with 12+ years of experience in the design industry. For the last 7 years at Parks + Associates, I've worked on visualizations that help people understand projects clearly before they're built. Prior to that, I spent 5 years at HBA Clark Philippines as a 2D Designer, developing a strong foundation in composition and detail.
